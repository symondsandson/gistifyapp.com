Gistify

The quickest and easiest way to share code on GitHub's Gist service. Exclusively available for Mac on the Mac App Store.

Global Hotkeys

Activate Gistify no matter where you are or what you're doing with a simple keyboard shortcut. It's never been simpler to share code with your colleagues and friends.

Use Your GitHub Account

Sign in to GitHub and share your gists through your username; or share anonymously either by default or on a gist-by-gist basis.

Override Defaults

Set sensible defaults that you use most of the time, and override them whenever you like with the "Gistify As" dialogue.
